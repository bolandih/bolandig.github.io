Bridging Finite Element and Deep Learning: High-Resolution Stress Distribution Prediction in Structural Components

We proposed deep neural networks in the form of convolutional neural networks (CNN) to bypass the FEA. The model is able to predict high-resolution stress distributions on loaded steel plates with variable loading, geometries and boundary conditions.
